Services and Solutions

Comprehensive product management services tailored to your specific needs and stage of growth.

Startup Founders Companies PM Teams Individuals Freelance

For Startup Founders

Product Co-Pilot 🚀

You don't need a full-time CPO to move fast and smart. You need a trusted partner who gets product and gets things done. This is your fractional product partner – hands-on, responsive, and focused on helping you ship faster with fewer mistakes.

  • • Product vision and strategy
  • • Define MVP scope, validate assumptions early
  • • Prioritize roadmap with business goals in mind
  • • Set up effective product workflows

For Companies

Product Strategy, Team Growth, Workshops

A strategic partner to help you build strong product teams and scale with clarity and focus. Whether you're hiring, training, or streamlining processes – I'm here to elevate your product organization.

  • • Hiring and talent support
  • • PM training and leadership mentoring
  • • Tailored workshops
  • • Product processes and methodology setup
  • • Interim product leadership
  • • Cross-functional alignment

For Teams

Product Operations, Process Excellence 🛠

Great PMs aren't just about talent – it's also about systems, rituals, and teamwork. I help teams:

  • • Streamline processes and team interfaces
  • • Select the right methods and tools
  • • Build effective templates
  • • Improve cross-org collaboration

For Individuals

1:1 Mentoring, Career Coaching 🪜

Personalized support to help you break into product, grow in your role, or land your next opportunity with confidence.

  • • From 0 to 1 – Land your first PM role
  • • What's Not Working? – You're experienced, but something's missing in interviews. We'll pinpoint the gaps and sharpen your story to land that next role.
  • • From Good to Great – Take your PM skills up a notch. Build leadership, clarity, and presence.

Freelance Product Work

Product Manager as a Service 🧩

For companies and startups that need a hands-on product lead – fast. I step in, adapt quickly, and move things forward. This isn't consulting – it's doing.

  • • Own product or initiative end-to-end
  • • Lead cross-functional delivery
  • • Backfill during hiring gaps or leaves
  • • Accelerate time-to-market with founders or execs
